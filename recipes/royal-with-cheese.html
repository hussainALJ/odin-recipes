Royal with Cheese

Home

Description

a hamburger that features one or more slices of cheese melted on top of the ground beef patty, typically served on a soft bun.
It's a customizable dish, often enhanced with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, and condiments
like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • ground beef patty
  • a bun
  • cheese
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • pickles

Steps

  1. Grill the ground beef patty.
  2. Put the grilled beef on the bun.
  3. Add cheese on top.
  4. Add the ketchup, mustard, and pickles on top.
